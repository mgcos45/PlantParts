Parts of a Plant

Explore the essential parts of a plant and their functions.

Root

The root anchors the plant in the soil, absorbs water and minerals, and stores food.

Stem

The stem supports the plant, transports nutrients and water between roots and leaves, and stores some nutrients.

Leaf

Leaves are the site of photosynthesis, where plants make food using sunlight, carbon dioxide, and water.

Flower

The flower is responsible for reproduction, attracting pollinators, and producing seeds for the next generation of plants.

Fruit

Fruits develop from flowers and protect seeds. They help in dispersal by attracting animals that eat and carry seeds.

Seed

Seeds contain the embryo of a new plant and hold nutrients needed for the plant's initial growth.
